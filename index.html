Welcome to the Digital Closet Organizer!

The Digital Closet Organizer is a virtual service that enables you to get the most out of your wardrobe. Record all items in your closet, including when and where purchased, number of times worn, and where you have it physically located. Share How Does It Fit tips with friends and build favorite ensembles. The Digital Closet Organizer can also remind you of garments and accessories you have tucked away. No longer be the victim of "out of sight, out of mind"!
